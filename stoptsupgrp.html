How To Stop Users Adding you in Whatsapp Groups

In this tutorial we will learn how to stop people from adding you to whatsapp groups it quite easy and fast.

If you don't want to be added in any group without your permission here you are in the right place.

NOTE:After you change to no one, still the group Adminis will be able to send you the invation links privately for that case you either agree or disagree to their request

Let's Go

Open your whatsapp

Then click on the three dots vertically arranged dots at the top right conner

Scroll down and tap on Settings

From settings tap on Account option. The datails of your profile will be displayed

From your account tap on Privacy

Then scroll down to Groups

After group option opens it will display

"Who can add me to Groups"

With the following below options

  1. Everyone
  2. My Contacts
  3. My Contacts except...

You can see the default was set everyone option

Now you can choose the option you want

Options Descriptions

If you live to Everyone it means

Anyone can join in the group he wants and that is the default option

If you choose My contacts means

In your contacts you have given anyone permission who wants to join you in a group, so this means only your nearby people can add in groups

If you choose My contacts execpt... means

In your contacts there people to whom you don't want to give them permission for adding you in groups you can choose them and leave the ones to have permission for adding you in groups

Now choose the option you want then click on DONE

Thats all Simple
